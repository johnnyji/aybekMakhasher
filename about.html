Cooking to me is a way of life. I had always wanted to be trained in a classical sense but later realized that focusing on a certain style restricts my creativity. Yes, a cusine represents a tradition that defines cultures time and again; but my philosophy is that of a one world cuisine.

I strongly believe in seasonal tastes, old school preserving methods and simplicity. Good food.
